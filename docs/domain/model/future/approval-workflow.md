# Approval Workflow

An approval workflow controls when a proposed change becomes official.

This concept is not required for the first version, but it is expected to become important when roles and permissions are introduced.

## Future Direction

A member may propose a change to a schedule, but the change only becomes visible to regular members after approval by the required validators.

Default validators may include organization administrators. Organizations may later define additional validators.

## Business Rule

Any change affecting the published schedule may require approval before becoming effective.
