# Invitation

An invitation allows an organization administrator to invite a user to join an organization or access a schedule.

## Business rules
- Only authorized members can invite other people to an organization.
- People are invited by email address.
- If someone has not registered yet, he will be able to see the invitation once he signs up.
- Someone who is already in the organization cannot be invited.

## Constraints

Email addresses must be normalized consistently before storage and comparison.

For now, invitations do not have to be kept. They can be deleted once they're accepted.
